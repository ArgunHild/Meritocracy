Analogies

You will see word pairs in the format: Word A : Word B = Word C : ?

Your task is to identify the relationship between A and B, then choose which of the 5 options (A–E) has the same relationship to C.

Example: pen : write = knife : ? → cut (a pen is used to write; a knife is used to cut).

In this learning trial you will answer 3 questions to get a feel for the task.
