Analogies

You will see word pairs in the format: Word A : Word B = Word C : ?

Your task is to identify the relationship between A and B, then choose which of the 5 options (A–E) has the same relationship to C.

In the real rounds you will have 1 minute and can answer up to 40 questions. In this learning trial you will answer 3 questions to get a feel for the task.

Example question — the correct answer here is C.

hot : cold = big : ?

"hot" is the opposite of "cold", so the answer is the opposite of "big" → small
