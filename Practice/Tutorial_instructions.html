Intelligence Test: Tutorial 🧠

  • In this experiment, you will perform at an intelligence test in multiple rounds.
  • Each round of the intelligence test will last 3 minutes and will consist of three blocks:
    1. Matrix reasoning: You will be shown a 3x3 matrix of shapes. Your task is to identify the missing shape in the bottom right corner.
    2. Analogies: You will be shown a pair of words with a certain relationship (e.g., "cat : kitten"). Then, you will be given a new word and asked to find the word that has the same relationship with it (e.g., "dog : _____" where the missing word would be "puppy").
    3. Mathematics: You will be given a series of mathematical problems to solve.

  • Each of these blocks will last 1 minute. After 1 minute, you will automatically move on to the next block. You may answer as many questions as you can within the time limit.
  • On the next few pages, you will play a tutorial of the intelligence test.
