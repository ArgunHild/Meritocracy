First rounds and Grouping

🧪

First rounds

  • In the next few pages, you will play two rounds of the Intelligence test.
  • In both of these rounds, one correct answer earns you {{C.Practice_ECs}} ECs.
    • Remember accumulated ECs will be exchanged into Euros at the end.
👥

Grouping

  • After the first two rounds, you will be grouped with 2 other participants.
  • Grouping is based on how you perform in these rounds.
  • At this point, we will not explain how grouping works, but it is in your interest to perform your best.
  • In other words, in addition to earning you ECs, your performance in the these rounds affect your earnings later in the experiment.
  • It is in your best interest to perform as well as you can.
