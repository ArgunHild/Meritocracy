Game 3 — Ultimatum

⚖️

How It Works

You will play two simultaneous ultimatum games — one against your Ingroup partner ({{ ingroup_tier_label }}) and one against your Outgroup partner ({{ outgroup_tier_label }}).

You have {{ dictator_ec }} ECs for each game.

Step 1 — Propose
Choose how much to keep and how much to offer to each partner (multiples of 10).
Step 2 — Respond
For each possible offer (0–100 ECs), decide whether you would accept or reject that offer from each partner.

If accepted: You keep your amount, partner receives your offer.
If rejected: Both of you earn 0 ECs from that game.

💡 Both steps happen simultaneously. Your total earnings = proposer earnings + responder earnings per game.
