How It Works

You will play two simultaneous games — one with your Partner A ({{ ingroup_tier_label }}) and one with your Partner B ({{ outgroup_tier_label }}).

You have {{ trust_ec }} ECs for each game.

Step 1 — Send
Choose how much to send to your partner (e.g., 0, 10, 30, etc.). The amount is tripled before it reaches them.
Step 2 — Receive and Return
For each amount your partner might send to you, decide how much of the tripled amount to return.
You will first play as the Sender then as the Receiver. Your partners will do the same. Your final earnings will depend on both your and your partners' decisions.

Your earnings as Sender: ({{ trust_ec }} ECs − amount you send) + amount returned to you
Your earnings as Receiver: (tripled received amount) − amount you return

💡 Total earnings per game = Earnings as Sender + earnings as Receiver.

📝

Example

You send 30 ECs → partner receives 90 ECs → partner returns 40 ECs.

  • Your Sender earnings: (50 − 30) + 40 = 60 ECs
  • Your Receiver earnings (if partner sent 20 → tripled to 60 → you return 20): 60 − 20 = 40 ECs
