Game 5 — Investment Game

🔄

Same game as in the Economy — but with strangers

This is the same cooperation game you played during the Economy phase. This time, you are matched with 2 completely anonymous participants from this session — not your Economy group members. You know nothing about them, and they know nothing about you.

💼

How It Works

You have {{ pgg2_commons }} ECs. Decide how many to invest in a common pool — any whole number from 0 to {{ pgg2_commons }} ECs.

  • The total invested by all 3 members is multiplied by 1.5 and divided equally among you.
  • You keep all ECs you did not invest, plus your equal share of the pool return.

Your earnings = ECs kept + (total pool × 1.5 ÷ 3)

If everyone invests 100 ECs:
Pool = 300 × 1.5 = 450 ÷ 3 = 150 ECs each
You kept 0 → Total: 150 ECs
If you invest 0, others invest 100 each:
Pool = 200 × 1.5 = 300 ÷ 3 = 100 ECs each
You kept 100 → Total: 200 ECs
