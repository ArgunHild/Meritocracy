Game 1 — Solidarity

🤝

How It Works

In this game, 2 out of 3 group members are randomly selected to receive a windfall of {{ solidarity_ec }} ECs. The third member receives nothing.

Before the draw, each member may pledge some of their potential windfall to the unlucky person. You decide on your pledge before knowing the outcome of the draw.

🍀 If you are LUCKY
You receive: {{ solidarity_ec }} ECs − your pledge
😔 If you are UNLUCKY
You receive: pledge from member 2 + pledge from member 3

💡 The pledge is made before anyone knows who will be lucky or unlucky. Each person in the group faces the same situation.

📝

Example

Suppose you pledge 10 ECs and your two group members each pledge 20 ECs.

  • If you are lucky: you earn {{ solidarity_ec }} − 10 = 90 ECs. Your pledged 10 ECs is transferred to the unlucky person.
  • If you are unlucky: you receive 20 + 20 = 40 ECs. Since you were unlucky, your pledged ECs are not taken from you.
