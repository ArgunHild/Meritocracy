A Simulated Economy

📋

Overview

This is the main part of the experiment. Your behavior in this part will have the largest effect on your bonus earnings.

You will now take part in 4 rounds of the simulated economy. Each round consists of three stages:

  1. Production — You will perform the intelligence test to produce ECs.
  2. Feedback — You will see how your performance and earnings compare to the others.
  3. Cooperation game — use a slider to decide how many tokens to place in the common pool.
👥

Groups

⚙️

Production

In the production stage, you will perform an intelligence test to earn ECs. Each correct answer will earn you {{C.Practice_ECs}} ECs. You can earn as many ECs as you want, but you only have 2 minutes to complete the test, so work quickly!

⚙️

Feedback

In the feedback stage, you will see how your performance and earnings compare to the others in your group. This information is used to inform your decisions in the cooperation game.

⚙️

Cooperation game

In the cooperation game, you will decide how many tokens to place in the common pool. The more tokens you contribute, the more you may earn in total.

🪙

Earnings

Your final bonus depends on the tokens you accumulate across all 4 rounds. Tokens you keep out of the pool are yours; tokens left in the pool may be shared or multiplied depending on group decisions.
