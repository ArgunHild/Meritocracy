In this game, 2 out of 3 group members will be randomly selected to win {{ solidarity_ec }} ECs. The third member receives nothing.

Before this happens, each group member may pledge some of their potential {{ solidarity_ec }} ECs to the person who doesn't win.

  • If you win, you keep your {{ solidarity_ec }} ECs minus your pledge: ({{ solidarity_ec }} ECs − your pledge).
  • If you don't win, you receive the pledges from both lucky members.

💡 You must decide how much to pledge before you know whether you win or don't win.
