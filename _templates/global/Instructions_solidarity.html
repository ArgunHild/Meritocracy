📋 Solidarity Game — Instructions

In this game, 2 out of 3 group members will be randomly selected to receive a windfall of {{ solidarity_ec }} ECs. The third member receives nothing.

Before the random draw, each group member may pledge some of their potential windfall to the unlucky person.

  • If you are lucky, you keep your windfall minus your pledge: ({{ solidarity_ec }} ECs − your pledge).
  • If you are unlucky, you receive the pledges from both lucky members.

💡 You must decide how much to pledge before you know whether you will be lucky or unlucky.
