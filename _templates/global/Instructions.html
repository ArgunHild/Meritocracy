What to Expect

In this experiment, you will perform at a real intelligence test. The experiment has three parts:

  1. First, you will play two practice rounds.
  2. Then, you will be grouped with 2 other participants. Here, you will perform the intelligence task separately and make some choices (More on this later).
  3. Finally, you will play simple economic games and answer some questions about yourself and your group.
Each part will be explained in detail before it begins.

How to earn bonus

  • Throughout the experiment, you will be earning ECs (Experiment Currency).
  • At the end of the experiment, your accumulated ECs will be converted to Euros at the following exchange rate: {{ C.EC_exchange_rate }} EC = 1 EUR .
    • For instance, if you earned 1500 ECs, you will be paid 15 EUR as bonus.
  • Different parts of the study have different rules for earning ECs. Before each part, we will explain the relevant rules.
