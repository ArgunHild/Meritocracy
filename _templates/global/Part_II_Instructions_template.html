A Simulated Economy

📋

Overview

This is the main part of the experiment — your earnings here will make up the largest share of your bonus payment.

You will take part in 4 rounds of a simulated economy. Each round has three stages:

  1. Competition — Perform the intelligence test. Your score determines your share of a pot of ECs.
  2. Feedback — See how your score and earnings compare to the other members of your Economy.
  3. Cooperation — Decide how much to contribute to a shared pool within your Economy.

Stage I: Competition

You will perform the full intelligence test — the same three blocks as before (Matrix Reasoning, Analogies, Mathematics), each lasting 1 minute.

Your score is calculated as:
 correct answers × your multiplier{{ welfare_bonus_text|safe }}

Your share of the {{ C.Economy_pie }} EC pot is proportional to your score within your Economy:
 Your ECs = (your score ÷ total group score) × {{ C.Economy_pie }}

Example: if your score is 50 and the others score 100 each, your share is (50 ÷ 250) × {{ C.Economy_pie }} = 100 ECs.

📊

Stage II: Feedback

After the competition, you will see how your performance compares to the others. You will see two bar charts showing:

  • Your correct answers compared to the correct answers of the other two.
  • Your earned ECs so far, compared to the earnings of the other two.

🤝

Stage III: Cooperation

Each member of your Economy receives {{ C.PGG_Commons }} ECs. You decide how many to place in a shared pool:

  • ECs you keep are added directly to your earnings.
  • ECs placed in the pool are multiplied by 1.5 and divided equally among all three members.

Because the pool multiplies contributions by 1.5, the group earns more in total when everyone contributes. However, since the pool is shared equally, each member also benefits from others' contributions regardless of their own contribution.

Example 1: if you contribute all 100 ECs to the pool, you earn 0 ECs from keeping. You and the others earn (100 × 1.5) ÷ 3 = 50 ECs from your contribution to the pool.
Example 2: if you contribute nothing but the others contribute 100 ECs each, you earn 100 ECs from keeping. You and the others each earn (300 × 1.5) ÷ 3 = 150 ECs from the pool.

🪙

Earnings

Your bonus from this part accumulates across all 4 rounds — both from the competition stage (your EC share of the pot) and from the cooperation stage (tokens kept and received from the pool).

All ECs are converted to Euros at the end of the experiment at the rate stated in the general instructions.
