This is the same "Group project" you played during previous Part.

You have {{ pgg2_commons }} ECs. Decide how many to invest in a common pool (any whole number from 0 to {{ pgg2_commons }} ECs).

  • The total invested by all 3 members is multiplied by 1.5 and divided equally among the three of you.
  • You keep all ECs you did not invest, plus your equal share of the pool return.

Your earnings = ECs kept + (total pool × 1.5 ÷ 3)

Examples:

Scenario Your earnings
Everyone invests 100 ECs Pool = 300 × 1.5 = 450 ÷ 3 = 150 ECs
You invest 0; others invest 100 each Kept 100 + pool share 100 = 200 ECs
You invest 50; others invest 50 each Kept 50 + (150 × 1.5 ÷ 3) = 50 + 75 = 125 ECs

💡 Investing more increases the total pot for everyone, but you personally keep less. Your slider lets you choose any amount from 0 to {{ pgg2_commons }} ECs.
